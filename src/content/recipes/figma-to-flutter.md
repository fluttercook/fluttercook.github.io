## What is figma-to-flutter?

A Dart code generator that converts Figma components to Flutter widgets. It focuses on building polished, reusable interface widgets faster. The project lives at [aloisdeniel/figma-to-flutter](https://github.com/aloisdeniel/figma-to-flutter) and is maintained by `aloisdeniel`.

## What is lotti?

Open-source private logbook with a local agentic layer. Long-living AI agents read what you record and propose what to do next. Hardware permitting, the models. It focuses on bringing on-device or cloud AI features into a Flutter app. The project lives at [matthiasn/lotti](https://github.com/matthiasn/lotti) and is maintained by `matthiasn`.

## What is liquid_glass_widgets?

Flutter UI kit implementing Apple's iOS 26 Liquid Glass design language - a comprehensive glass widget library with real shader-based blur, physics-driven jelly. It focuses on adding smooth, expressive motion to your Flutter app. The project lives at [sdegenaar/liquid_glass_widgets](https://github.com/sdegenaar/liquid_glass_widgets) and is maintained by `sdegenaar`.

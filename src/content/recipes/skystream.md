## What is skystream?

SkyStream is a modern, cross-platform media streaming client inspired by CloudStream. It uses a custom JavaScript runtime for plugins, allowing users to scrape. It focuses on improving your Flutter developer workflow and codebase. The project lives at [akashdh11/skystream](https://github.com/akashdh11/skystream) and is maintained by `akashdh11`.

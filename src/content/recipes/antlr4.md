## What is antlr4?

ANTLR (ANother Tool for Language Recognition) is a powerful parser generator for reading, processing, executing, or translating structured text or binary files. It focuses on improving your Flutter developer workflow and codebase. The project lives at [antlr/antlr4](https://github.com/antlr/antlr4) and is maintained by `antlr`.

## Installing antlr4

Add the package to your `pubspec.yaml`:

```yaml

  antlr4: ^latest
```

Then fetch it and import it in your Dart code:

```bash

```

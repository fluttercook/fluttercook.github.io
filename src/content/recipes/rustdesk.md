## What is rustdesk?

An open-source remote desktop application designed for self-hosting, as an alternative to TeamViewer. It focuses on building polished, reusable interface widgets faster. The project lives at [rustdesk/rustdesk](https://github.com/rustdesk/rustdesk) and is maintained by `rustdesk`.

## What is drawing_animation?

A Flutter library for gradually painting SVG path objects on canvas (drawing line animation). It focuses on adding smooth, expressive motion to your Flutter app. The project lives at [haucky/drawing_animation](https://github.com/haucky/drawing_animation) and is maintained by `haucky`.

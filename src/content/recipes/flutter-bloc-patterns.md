## What is flutter-bloc-patterns?

A set of most common BLoC use cases built on top of flutter_bloc library. It focuses on keeping your UI in sync with your app state as it changes. The project lives at [klisiewicz/flutter-bloc-patterns](https://github.com/klisiewicz/flutter-bloc-patterns) and is maintained by `klisiewicz`.

## What is nhost?

The Open Source Firebase Alternative with GraphQL. It focuses on talking to APIs, databases, and persistence layers. The project lives at [nhost/nhost](https://github.com/nhost/nhost) and is maintained by `nhost`.

## When should you use nhost?

Reach for nhost when:

- you're calling REST/GraphQL APIs from a Flutter app
- you need local storage, caching, or offline sync
- you want typed, testable data access

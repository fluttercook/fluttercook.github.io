## What marionette_mcp is

MCP server enabling AI agents to interact with Flutter apps at runtime - let them inspect widgets, simulate taps, enter text, scroll, and take screenshots. It lives at [leancodepl/marionette_mcp](https://github.com/leancodepl/marionette_mcp) and is maintained by the `leancodepl` project.

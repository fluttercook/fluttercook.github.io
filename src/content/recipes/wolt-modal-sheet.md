## What wolt_modal_sheet is

This package provides a responsive modal with multiple pages, motion animation for page transitions, and scrollable content within each page. It lives at [woltapp/wolt_modal_sheet](https://github.com/woltapp/wolt_modal_sheet) and is maintained by the `woltapp` project.

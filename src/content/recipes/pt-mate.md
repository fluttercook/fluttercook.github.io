## What is pt_mate?

基于 Flutter（Material Design 3）开发的私有种子站点客户端，支持多种PT站点的种子浏览、搜索和下载管理。目前支持M-Team和NexusPHP类型的站点。. It focuses on building polished, reusable interface widgets faster. The project lives at [JustLookAtNow/pt_mate](https://github.com/JustLookAtNow/pt_mate) and is maintained by `JustLookAtNow`.

## What is android-ir-blaster?

Create custom infrared (IR) remotes using hex codes, raw signals, or import Flipper Zero, LIRC, and IRPLUS files. It focuses on keeping your UI in sync with your app state as it changes. The project lives at [iodn/android-ir-blaster](https://github.com/iodn/android-ir-blaster) and is maintained by `iodn`.

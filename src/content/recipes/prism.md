## What is Prism?

Prism is an open-source Flutter wallpapers and home screen setups app for Android and iOS, with community uploads, curated collections, and AI wallpaper generat. It focuses on bringing on-device or cloud AI features into a Flutter app. The project lives at [Hash-Studios/Prism](https://github.com/Hash-Studios/Prism) and is maintained by `Hash-Studios`.

## Installing Prism

Add the package to your `pubspec.yaml`:

```yaml

  Prism: ^latest
```

Then fetch it and import it in your Dart code:

```bash
flutter pub get
```
